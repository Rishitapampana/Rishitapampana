Main Characters

Hades: The enigmatic god of the underworld, known for his powerful presence and complex personality. He struggles with his feelings for Persephone.
Persephone: The goddess of spring who finds herself torn between her duties and her desires. Her journey of self-discovery is central to the story.
Zeus: The king of the gods, whose decisions impact the lives of all. His authoritative demeanor often clashes with his complex emotions.
Poseidon: The god of the sea, known for his fierce loyalty and strong sense of justice. He plays a crucial role in the dynamics between gods.
